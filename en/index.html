We help you better understand your body and take care of your health – step by step

1

Profile and personalization

Create an account and complete your data to receive an individually tailored rehabilitation plan. The app analyzes your age, height and surgery date to create a safe and effective exercise calendar.

2

Range of motion test

Perform a simple test in front of the camera to check your hand's capabilities. The app will record your range of motion and adapt exercises to your needs - without overload and risk of injury.

3

Exercises with motion analysis

Exercise safely with the help of a camera and intelligent system that tracks the correctness of your movements. Each exercise includes instructions, animation and real-time feedback.

4

Statistics and progress

Track your progress day by day. Check the number of exercises performed, quality of movements and changes in range - all presented in clear charts.
